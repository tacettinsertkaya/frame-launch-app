Canva vs Frame Launch: which is better for app screenshots?

Canva is a broad design tool. Frame Launch is a focused screenshot generator for app launches.

Use Canva when

You need a general-purpose design system, brand templates, presentation assets, or a team library that goes beyond app screenshots.

Use Frame Launch when

You already have product screenshots and need to turn them into App Store, Google Play, Product Hunt, Open Graph, or social assets quickly. Frame Launch includes launch-focused sizes, device framing, captions, backgrounds, and batch export.

Practical difference

In Canva, you start from a blank design surface. In Frame Launch, you start from the screenshot workflow: upload app screens, pick output size, write the caption, style the device, and export.
