When Frame Launch is a better fit

Choose Frame Launch when your priority is speed, no signup, no watermark, and keeping screenshots on your device. The editor runs in the browser, stores projects locally, and gives indie teams a focused workflow for App Store, Google Play, social, and launch page assets.

What you get

  • Browser-only editing with local project storage.
  • Device frames, backgrounds, captions, and batch ZIP export.
  • Common store and marketing sizes without a design handoff.
  • A privacy-forward workflow for unreleased app screens.

Positioning

AppLaunchpad-style tools can be useful for broader store listing workflows. Frame Launch is intentionally narrower: open the editor, create screenshot visuals, export assets, and keep moving.
