Comparison

Figma templates vs Frame Launch: choose the workflow that ships faster.

Figma templates are flexible. Frame Launch is focused on screenshot creation and export.

Use Figma templates when

You already work in Figma, have a brand system, and want complete design control across many asset types.

Use Frame Launch when

You need to create app screenshots without setting up frames, constraints, export presets, or template variants manually. Frame Launch gives you the core controls in one browser editor.

Practical launch tradeoff

Templates can become another design project. Frame Launch is better when the job is narrow: get screenshots ready for a store page, social post, Product Hunt gallery, or launch page today.
