Why teams compare AppScreens and Frame Launch

Both categories solve the same launch problem: turning product screenshots into persuasive store visuals. Frame Launch is built for teams that want a lightweight, free, privacy-forward editor instead of a managed account-based workflow.

Frame Launch strengths

  • No account required for the core editor.
  • No watermark on PNG exports.
  • Screenshot data stays local in the browser.
  • Fast iteration for App Store, Google Play, and social assets.

Best use case

Use Frame Launch when you need to prepare or revise a screenshot set in the same session: upload, caption, style, export, and ship.
