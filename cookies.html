Cookies

Browser storage keeps the app useful without accounts.

Frame Launch uses local browser storage for editor state and may use advertising cookies only after ads are enabled and consent requirements are met.

Strictly necessary storage

The editor uses IndexedDB to save your projects, screenshots, localized images, and design settings. It uses localStorage for preferences such as theme, active tab, language, and optional API keys you add for AI features. This storage is necessary for the editor experience.

Functional third-party requests

When you use certain features, the browser may request Google Fonts, CDN-hosted JavaScript libraries, or AI provider APIs. These services may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Advertising storage

Ads are currently disabled by default. When AdSense is configured, Google may use cookies, local storage, web beacons, and other identifiers to serve and measure ads. Personalized ads will only run where permitted and after required consent choices are available.

Managing choices

You can clear Frame Launch data from your browser settings at any time. Doing so removes saved projects and preferences. If ads are enabled, visitors will be given ad privacy choices in regions where consent is required.
