About

A free screenshot studio for app launches.

Frame Launch helps indie developers, founders, and small teams create polished app store and launch visuals without handing their screenshots to a hosted design service.

Why it exists

App makers often need good screenshots before they have a designer, budget, or marketing system. Frame Launch keeps that workflow simple: upload screenshots, choose a market-ready size, add text, style the frame, and export PNG files for stores or launch pages.

What makes it different

The editor is free, browser-based, and account-free. Projects save locally, so private app screenshots stay on your device. The product also supports localized screenshot images and multi-language text so teams can prepare more than one market from the same project.

How it will stay free

Frame Launch plans to use advertising on public content pages, guides, and templates. The editor itself stays clean for launch, with no ad inside the canvas, upload, modal, or export workflow.

Open source roots

The project is MIT licensed and built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML Canvas, Three.js, IndexedDB, and CSS. The production website adds trust pages, launch guides, and ad infrastructure around the existing editor.
