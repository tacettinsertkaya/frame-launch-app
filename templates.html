Templates

Free screenshot layout ideas for app launches.

Use these patterns as starting points inside Frame Launch. Pick the message structure first, then adjust color, device frame, background, and text for your product.

Template gallery

These templates are intentionally described as workflows rather than locked files. That keeps them flexible for App Store, Google Play, social cards, and launch-page hero images.

SaaS dashboard

Lead with a crisp dashboard screenshot, a one-line outcome, and two small proof badges such as "Realtime reports" and "Export-ready."

AI assistant

Use a chat or result screen, then frame the value as speed, clarity, or automation. Keep prompt text short enough to read on mobile.

Fitness tracker

Show progress, streak, or workout detail screens. Use energetic color, but keep the data legible and the caption focused on habit formation.

Finance utility

Use restrained backgrounds, clear numbers, and a privacy-forward caption. Avoid visual clutter around balances, charts, or exchange rates.

Creator tool

Show the before and after state. Put the output, not the settings panel, at the center of the screenshot.

Local service app

Use map, booking, or schedule screens with a caption that explains the practical result: find, book, save, share, or arrive.

How to use a template in Frame Launch

  1. Open the editor and upload screenshots in story order.
  2. Choose the output size for the store or marketing channel.
  3. Write the headline before styling the background.
  4. Transfer the first screenshot style to the rest of the set.
  5. Export one proof PNG, then export the full ZIP.
