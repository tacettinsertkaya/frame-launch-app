FAQ

Answers before you upload a screenshot

Frame Launch is designed for early app teams that need fast, private, launch-ready assets.
Do my screenshots upload to your servers?

No. Screenshots are processed in your browser and projects are stored locally with IndexedDB.

Is it really free and watermark-free?

Yes. The editor is free, account-free, and does not place a watermark on exported images.

Can I use exports for App Store, Google Play, and ads?

Yes. Exported PNGs can be used for store listings, launch pages, Product Hunt, social posts, and paid campaigns.

Does it support localization?

Yes. You can manage language-specific text and localized screenshots for multi-market launch sets.

What if I need exact platform sizes?

The editor includes common device and marketing sizes, including App Store, Google Play, Open Graph, Twitter Card, and custom exports.

Does the editor work on mobile?

The landing page is mobile-friendly. The full editor is optimized for desktop and large tablet screens because screenshot composition needs space.
